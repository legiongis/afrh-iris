About AFRH-IRIS

The online Armed Forces Retirement Home Information and Resource Inventory System (AFRH-IRIS) offers a new and exciting way to explore and manage the historic resources of the Armed Forces Retirement Home - Washington.

  • Search the Map to discover our resources and understand the layout of the AFRH-W grounds.
  • Check out the historic maps that we have included as transparent overlays.
  • Use the search bar to find specific historic resources, photos, and people.
  • Every page has built-in help in case you get stuck--just click the ? link at the top of the page.
Start Exploring

This website is powered by Arches v5, a free and open source software platform designed to create and manage rich inventories of culturally signficant resources. Check out the Arches website to learn more, or the Arches forum to get involved.
